PROMPT FORMULA (MANDATORY for every prompt)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Every prompt MUST contain all 5 elements in this order:

  [SPECIFIC SUBJECT] + [EMOTIONAL STATE / ACTION] + [LIGHTING] + [COMPOSITION] + [VISUAL STYLE]

GOOD — all 5 elements, specific and visual:
  "elderly philosopher seated at a rain-streaked window at midnight, head bowed in quiet despair, cold blue moonlight from the left casting long shadows, tight medium shot framing face and hands, oil painting baroque realism"

  "two hands reaching toward each other across a void, fingertips almost touching, warm golden backlight from below, extreme close-up shallow depth of field, renaissance fine art oil painting"

  "vast Roman forum at dawn seen from above, senators in togas forming small clusters, raking golden morning light across marble columns, wide aerial establishing shot, epic classical art style"

BAD — too abstract, too generic, missing elements:
  "philosophical concept about fear"           ← no subject, no lighting, no composition
  "man thinking about his past"                ← no setting, no emotion, no style
  "dark and moody atmosphere"                  ← no subject at all
  "figure in a baroque chapel"                 ← generic, no emotion, no specific action

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
NARRATION LOCK RULE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Each prompt must illustrate the SPECIFIC idea in the surrounding narration — not the general topic.

A viewer who sees the image must be able to guess what sentence is being spoken.

WRONG: narration says "the shadow holds everything we rejected about ourselves"
  → prompt: "ancient scholar at a desk" (illustrates Jung, not shadow/rejection)

RIGHT: narration says "the shadow holds everything we rejected about ourselves"
  → prompt: "person standing before a cracked mirror showing a darker distorted reflection of themselves, moment of disturbing self-recognition, cold blue moonlight from the side, full-frame confrontation with reflection, surrealist oil painting realism"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
UNIQUENESS RULES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Track ALL prompts you have written so far in this session.
No two prompts may share more than one of:
  - Same subject type (e.g., two "solitary figures at windows")
  - Same setting type (e.g., three "empty libraries")
  - Same lighting (e.g., four "cold blue moonlight" scenes)
  - Same composition (e.g., five "wide aerial shots")

Vary systematically across the full video:
  - Subjects: person, landscape, object, symbolic space, crowd, hands/body parts, architecture
  - Settings: interior, exterior, natural, urban, dreamlike, abstract, historical
  - Lighting: golden hour, harsh midday, candlelight, cold blue, silhouette, diffuse overcast, dramatic chiaroscuro
  - Composition: extreme close-up, medium shot, wide shot, aerial, low angle, silhouette, rule of thirds
  - Emotional register: contemplative, tense, hopeful, cathartic, eerie, warm, confrontational

VISUAL ARC
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The video has an emotional arc. Your images must follow it:

  OPENING (first 20% of markers): Bold, attention-grabbing, slightly unsettling or mysterious

  RESOLUTION (last 30%): Warmer, more hopeful, lighter — visual catharsis

Do not use warm golden light in the opening. Do not use cold blue in the closing.
